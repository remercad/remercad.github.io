Introduction

The Gallery

The Gallery was started in 2019 by entrepreneur Regan Mercado. Beginning by selling her jewelry online as a small business, The Gallery gained immense popularity. Only a year after Regan was able to open her first store in 2020 and is still growing her business today. Driven by her love for jewelry and design, she has taken her passions to craft beautiful and timeless pieces for your jewelry box.

Target audience

My target audience for this website is mainly toward women who are into fashion and jewelry. The age demographic would mainly be 20–55 years old who are part of the middle class. They value a quality product and are willing to spend a little more to obtain it. The audience is looking for beautiful jewelry that is easy to shop physically and online. The target audience may want to purchase this product for themselves or as a gift for friends and family.

Their goals are to purchase quality jewelry that is reasonably priced. Their primary task is to easily navigate the e-commerce website to make their purchase.
